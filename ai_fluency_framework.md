## Anthropic Academy - AI Fluency: Framework & Foundations

- Focus on human-AI collaboration

### AI Fluency
- Devloping a collection of practial skills, knowledge, insights and values that reinforce eachother and adapt as the technology changes.
    - Effective
    - Efficient
    - Ethical
    - Safe
 - Automation: You know the task, the AI performs it.
 - Augmentation: Collaborate with the AI, work together to arrive at the final plan.
 - Agency: AI works independently on your behalf.

##### All serve a different purpose and excel in different situaitons. Could use all three in one project. Augmentation and Agency take advantage of the unique capabilities of AI, can lead to the most creative and effective solution.

### 4D Framework
- Delegation
    - Understand the goal and problem you are trying to solve.
    - Know limits and capabilities of AI systems
    - Decide how to divide work between you and AI.
- Description: Clear communication with AI
    - Beyond just writing prompts. Detailed, context rich conversations:
        - Final output
        - How AI should approach the task
        - How shouldt the AI behave?
- Discernment: Thoguhtfully evaluating what AI gives you
    - Does the reasoning make sense?
    - Are the "facts" accurate?
    - Does this help me achieve my goal?
- Diligence: Responsible AI interactions
    - Ensuring accuracy
    - Honesty and Transparency
    - Are you willing to take responsibility and stand behind what your AI created?

### Deep Dive: What Is Generative AI?
#### Fundamentals:
- Create new content vs just analyzing traditional data. (Identify spam email vs write a complete email for you)
- Three Pillars made GenAI Possible
    - Algorithms
        - Nueral Networks
        - Transformers
    - Data
        - Articles and websites
        - Code and multimodal content
    - Computation
        - GPUs, TPUs
        - Computing clusters
- Scaling Laws: As models grew larger and were trained on more data along with higher compute, their behavior was predicatble.
- Context window: The AIs workking memory
    - Prompts
    - AI Responses
    - Any other info you've shared
- What makes GenAI powerful?
    - Process vast information
    - Adapts to new tasks through in-context learning
    - Demonstrates emergent capabilities from scale
#### Capabilities and Limitations
- Capabilities
    - Write emails
    - Converse about complex topics
- Limitations
    - Knowledge cutoff date
    - Some incorrect information (learn and reproduce inaccuracies)
        - This leads to a "hallucination"
    - AI context window. How much can the AI consider in a single interaction?
    - Non-deterministic outputs.
        - Temperature is the ability to adjust the randomness of accuracy.
#### Delegation
- Deciding what work is to be done by who
- Know eachothers strengths/weakness
- Most effective collaborators are expertes in their field first
