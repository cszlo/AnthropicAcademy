# AI Fluency Framework — Quiz Review

## Q1 ✓ What does AI Fluency mean?
**The ability to work with AI effectively, efficiently, ethically, and safely**

## Q2 ✓ The 4 Ds of the AI Fluency Framework
**Delegation, Description, Discernment, Diligence**

## Q3 ✓ Which competency focuses on deciding what work AI vs. humans should do?
**Discernment**

## Q4 ✓ Primary focus of the Diligence competency?
**Responsible AI use, transparency, and accountability**

## Q5 ✓ "Problem Awareness" within Delegation?
**Clearly defining your goals and understanding what work is needed before involving AI**

## Q6 ✓ Which competency focuses on communicating clearly with AI about what you want?
**Description**

## Q7 ✓ "Product Description" within the Description competency?
**Clearly defining what you want the AI to create or provide**

## Q8 ✓ Which competency involves critically evaluating AI outputs, processes, and behaviors?
**Discernment**

## Q9 ✗ "Process Discernment" within the Discernment competency?
**Correct answer: Assessing whether the AI's reasoning process and problem-solving approach is effective**
*(Not: Deciding which AI system to use for a task)*

## Q10 ✓ "Transparency Diligence" within the Diligence competency?
**Being open and honest about AI's role in your work with those who need to know**
